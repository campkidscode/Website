Our Mission

Camp KidsCode is an nonprofit organization pending incorporation. We are dedicated to increasing access to a computer science education through free and fun classes, both online and in person. Middle school years are especially important for children when deciding their career goals and we want to be able to reach out to them at this time. In our classes, we make programming fun and relatable by emphasizing the use of programming in almost any field, from animation to design to business.

Why learn to program?

Learning to program empowers kids to express their creativity in new and amazing ways! The possibilities are almost limitless with just a text-editor and an internet connection.

Within the next ten years, programming will be one of the fastest growing occupations. According to kodable.org, a computer science education organization, over the nexxt ten years, there will be 1.4 million programming jobs to fill and 1 million of them will go unfilled.

Moreover, over 67% of these jobs will be outside the tech sector, emphasizing the need for an interdisciplinary and accessible programming education.
