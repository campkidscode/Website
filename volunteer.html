Volunteer!

Camp KidsCode is looking for volunteers to help run our online classes and daily operations! If you would like to help, please read the position descriptions below and fill out the form to the right.

Mentors

A flexible position requiring a few hours a week to prep and teach one of the paths on our online class. You will be working with 5-6 middle school students. This might require some outside prep to plan and test the activities you will be teaching but it is definitely the most fun!

Speakers

Our speakers help the students understand the applications of the concepts they are learning about. If you would like the change to reach out our middle-school participants but cannot make the same time commitment as a mentor, participate as a speaker and take them through a day in the life of an engineer/computer scientist.

Social Media Expert

Social media is an especially important way to reach out to potential students as well as showcase the cool things our current students are building. You would be expected to update our Facebook and Twitter accounts before/after every class as well as sharing interesting current events in the tech world. This is a role involving a fair amount of independent action.
